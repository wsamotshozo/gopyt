How we work




  • Step one: Build a profile and share with everyone

    • With GOPYT (‘GO Pay Your Tuition’), current students are able to sign up and build a profile with pictures, videos and messages reaching out to friends and family all over the world to come and help contribute to their higher education goals. GOPYT has all the resources for you to network the people you need. You as a student will select your current high school or college when you build your profile. This way GOPYT is able to track your progress in terms of the total amount you raise and rank your success. This will allow you to earn additional prizes and cash for your goals. This means, if you work hard and raise money, you will earn additional rewards that will go towards your pool of tuition funds.

  • Step Two: Work hard and build supporters

    • As a student you should work hard to remind supporters to send your profile to additional friends and family and let them know that you have a chance to earn more than just what you personally collect. You will need to update your profile, and even reach out to organizations and your community to rally support to help fund your education.

  • Step Three: Raise the most money and win the race

    • So for example, if all the students at your school have a profile, GOPYT groups all of these students into one pool. The student at the end of the month with the most money earned in that month will win a pool of cash and prizes. Because hey, we all know how students love a little competition, right? The current pot that’s available to be won will be updated in real time so you’ll always know what is at stake. Also, your friends and family will be able to follow your progress each month and help keep you in that number one spot. It is also up to you to reach out and keep people updated on your monthly ranking. At the end of the month if you’ve raised the most money, YOU WIN that months prize (it’s that simple).

  • Step Four : Say thank you by helping your community

    • As a great big thank you to everyone who’s helped you along the way, for every thousand dollars that you raise, you can show your gratitude by committing to helping your local community or non-profit organization as a way to give back to everyone that is supporting your journey. This will be a great way to get and keep support from people that can see you giving up your time to help fund your education and be a leader in the civilian community. So for example, let’s say one of our students is passionate about the "Stand up for Cancer Society" and their family supports this organization. If they then raise $20,000 total through GOPYT, they could then repay the local community that has supported them by helping to work with the "Stand up for Cancer Society" or other service projects. One cool twist about this feature is that we push for you to help coordinate and support big and local celebrities’ organizations to gain a larger support network. Mariah Carey and Alicia Keys were recently spotted supporting "Stand Up for Cancer". By supporting organizations such as this, students also get the chance to widen their network of supporters. Who knows, one day you might just be lucky enough to have Alicia or Mariah helping to fund your college education!

  • Step Five: Fund your education and graduate

    • By using our platforms and with GOPYT’s help, students can actively overcome their financial burdens and fully fund their higher education. This will allow you to walk out of school on graduation day not only with a degree, but also with massively reduced debts and, perhaps most importantly, with the knowledge that you’ve achieved it all by giving back to your community and helping others. Then of course there’s the added bonus that you can use GOPYT's platform to fund your education quicker with the chance to win monthly incentives. There’s only really one question left to ask: “Where do I sign up?”
